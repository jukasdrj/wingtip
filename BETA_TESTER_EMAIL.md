## Getting Started

1. **Install TestFlight** from the App Store (if you don't have it already)
2. **Open the invitation link** sent in a separate email from Apple
3. Tap **Accept** and then **Install**
4. **Launch Wingtip** from your home screen

The app will walk you through a quick onboarding and ask for camera permission.

## What is Wingtip?

Wingtip is an iOS-first app designed for book lovers who want to:
- Quickly catalog their physical book collection
- Search and browse their library offline
- Track book statistics and collections
- Maintain complete privacy with local-first storage

All your book data is stored locally on your device. Images are temporarily uploaded to our AI service for identification, then immediately deleted after processing.

## What We Need You to Test

Please focus on these key areas during your beta testing:

### 1. Book Scanning
- **Test the camera scanning**: Point your camera at book spines and tap the shutter button
- **Try different conditions**: Good lighting, low light, various spine styles
- **Test multiple books**: Scan at least 10-15 books to get a feel for accuracy
- **Note any issues**: Blurry results, incorrect identifications, crashes

### 2. Library Management
- **Browse your library**: Scroll through your book collection
- **Use search**: Try searching by title, author, and ISBN
- **Edit book details**: Tap a book and try editing its information
- **Delete books**: Test removing books from your library

### 3. Offline Mode
- **Turn off Wi-Fi**: Disable internet and test browsing your library
- **Test search offline**: Ensure full-text search works without connection
- **Try scanning offline**: Verify the app shows appropriate feedback

### 4. Performance & UI
- **Note any lag**: Especially during scrolling or search
- **Watch for crashes**: Any unexpected exits or freezes
- **UI feedback**: Does everything feel responsive and intuitive?
- **Report visual bugs**: Missing borders, incorrect colors, layout issues

## How to Send Feedback

### Method 1: Shake-to-Feedback (Recommended)
1. **Shake your device** anywhere in the app
2. A feedback form will appear automatically
3. Describe what happened
4. Tap **Send** - device info and logs are included automatically

### Method 2: Via Debug Menu
1. From the Camera screen, tap the **gear icon** (top-left)
2. Scroll to **"Send Feedback"** section
3. Tap **"Send Feedback"** button
4. Fill out the form and tap **Send**

### Method 3: TestFlight App
1. Open the **TestFlight** app
2. Select **Wingtip**
3. Tap **"Send Beta Feedback"**
4. Include screenshots if relevant

## Known Issues

We're aware of these issues and are working on fixes:

- **First camera load**: May take 1-2 seconds on first launch
- **Search performance**: Large collections (>500 books) may see slight delays
- **Rate limiting**: Rapid scanning (~20+ scans in a row) may trigger rate limits
- **Cover image caching**: First time loading cover images may be slow

Please still report these if you encounter them - we want to know how frequently they occur!

## Testing Goals

We're aiming to answer these questions through your testing:

1. **Accuracy**: How often does the book identification work correctly?
2. **Performance**: Does the app feel fast and responsive?
3. **Stability**: Are there any crashes or major bugs?
4. **Usability**: Is the interface intuitive? Any confusing flows?
5. **Edge cases**: What happens with unusual book spines, damaged covers, etc?

## Privacy & Data

- All book data is stored **locally on your device**
- Images are **temporarily uploaded** for AI processing only
- We **immediately delete** images after processing
- No personal data is collected except:
  - Anonymous device ID (for rate limiting)
  - Crash reports (via Sentry, opt-in)
  - Feedback you explicitly send

You can view your device ID and export your library data anytime from the Debug Settings menu.
